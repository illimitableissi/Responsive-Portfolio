About Me



Hello, My name is Isimemen Inegbedion. I am a Nigerian blood American born and raised in Atlanta, GA. My life has been quite the journey so far when it comes to education. I originally went to college to work in the field of Psychology. It took for me to get to my senior year before I came to the conclusion that my life should not continue towards that path. I took an intro to Accounting course in my senior year and figured Accounting was not a bad career to pursue. I immediately enrolled back into college to pursue a degree in Accounting. It took slightly longer than I anticipated to graduate but I was able to obtain my BBA in Accounting and start my career.


Three years into my career in the Accounting field I found myself at a crossroads. In order to advance my career I would need to obtain my CPA as well as enrolling in grad school, two things I have no real interest in pursueing. While consulting with some close friends on what I should do with my life I was introduced to IT and web development. I decided to take the plunge and now i'm here, very eager to learn and upgrade my life.
